RUNNING THE GAME:

On Windows or Mac OS X, locate the "run_game.pyw" file and double-click it.

Othewise open a terminal / console and "cd" to the game directory and run:

  python run_game.py
  (if your system reports it cannot find Python, please research how to
   update your system-path to reflect Python's installation location.)


HOW TO PLAY THE GAME:


Firstly, thanks for playing. The objective in 
Lawn & Order is simple. Maintain your lawn 
while you destory your neighbor's. There are
several level-based hazards so keep up on 
that lawn-care!

                       Player 1              Player 2 
    = CONTROLS ------+--------------------------------+=
    LEFT / RIGHT     |    A, D                 J,L
    PICKUP / DROP    |    S                    K
    THROW / ACTIVATE |    W                    I

    - Strategy -
    Move close to an item to pick it up.
    
    Hold throw to increase launch velocity.
    
    Heal your lawn with the Watering Can.
    
    Use the game entities to your advantage.
